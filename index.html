What I'm planning on doing after college/h2>

I am going to Florida Southern College to study cybersecurity. After that, I'm going to put my feelers out near the college and go from there.

My eventual plan is to go work for Google's Project Zero. Google formed the cybersecurity task force after a vulnerability in Microsoft's Internet Explorer 6 allowed Google to be compromised. This is the only time Google has been breached, which is a very impressive achievement considering how lucrative of a target they must be. Project Zero ensures that Google and its users won't get screwed over based off of another company's negligence. Either that, or I'll go work for SpaceX and hopefully go to space. Elon call me up.

My plans since I wrote that paragraph back in 2019 have definitely changed. Similarly to most people, my love of space has been largely reingited by SpaceX's Demo-2 crewed launch to the International Space Station. As such, I'm aspiring to work at SpaceX in cybersecurity, probably on the Starlink program (have you ever heard of a Falcon 9 getting hacked? Me neither).
